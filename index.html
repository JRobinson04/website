Aunt Karen's Funnel Cake

Ingredients

  1. 1 quart vegtable oil for frying, or as needed
  2. 1 1/2 cups milk
  3. 2 large eggs
  4. 2 cups all-purpose flour
  5. 1 teaspoon baking powered
  6. 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  7. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  8. 3/4 cup powdered sugar

Directions:

1. Heat oil to 375 degrees F (190 degree C) in a heavy skillet or deep fryer. 3. Use your finger to cover the funnel hole; pour about 1 cup of batter into the funnel. Starting in the centre of the skillet, remove your finger and move the funnel in a swirling motion to make a 6- or 7- inch round. Fry in the hot oil until bottom is golden brown; flip and continue cooking until golden brown all over, about 1 minute per sid. Drain on paper towels. Continue cooking remaining batter. 4. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and serve warm. 2. Beat milk and eggs together in a large bowl. Combine flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt in a seperate bowl; stir into egg mixture until smooth. Funnel cakes are best fresh but can be kept in refridgerator for 3 days or frozen for upto 3 months. Reheat the cover with powdered sugar.
